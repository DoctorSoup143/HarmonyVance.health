FAQ

How long have you been in practice?

I have been in practice since October of 2018 after graduating from South University of Tampa after completing their longest program to become a Family Nurse Practitioner. I treat patients of all ages, backgrounds, sexes, and genders- focusing on comprehensive personalized medical treatment.

What states do you provide telehealth services in?

As a nurse practitioner, I currently only provide telehealth services to the states listed below.

Arizona, Arkansas, Colorado, Connecticut, District of Columbia, Delaware, Florida, Iowa, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Massachusetts, Maryland, Maine, Montana, North Dakota, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Mexico, Nevada, New York, Oregon, Rhode Island, South Dakota, Utah, West Virginia, and Wyoming.

Do you take insurance?

Currently, no. The platform I utilize to communicate with patients, Push Health, currently does not allow me to accept payment from insurance companies. Instead, I charge a flat fee of $84.99 for my services, consultation, and for a prescription.

What if insurance wont cover my medication?

Once a determination is received by your insurance company regarding a denial of coverage for a drug, we still have a few options left. Depending on the drug I may be able to prescribe it to you at a fixed out of pocket rate and have it shipped to your doorstep!

This result varies depending on the requested medication.

Do you offer compound medication options?

Yes! When it comes to weight loss I offer compounds such as Semaglutide and Tirzepatide with B6, in order to assist with nausea. These medications can be shipped to your doorstep at no extra charge, if this route is medically appropriate, complete with administration and storage instructions.
