FAQ

How long have you been in practice?

I have been in practice since October of 2018 after graduating from South University of Tampa and completing their longest program to become a Family Nurse Practitioner. I treat patients of all ages, backgrounds, sexes, and genders- focusing on comprehensive personalized medical treatment.

What states do you provide telehealth services in?

As a nurse practitioner, I currently only provide telehealth services to the states listed below.

Arizona, Arkansas, California, Colorado, Connecticut, District of Columbia, Delaware, Florida, Iowa, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Louisiana, Massachusetts, Maryland, Maine, Michigan, Montana, North Carolina, North Dakota, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Mexico, Nevada, New York, Oregon, Rhode Island, South Carolina, South Dakota, Utah, West Virginia, Wisconsin, and Wyoming.

Do you take insurance?

Currently, no. We do not participate in insurance programs, and our treatments are not eligible for Medicare reimbursement. The platform I utilize to communicate with patients, Push Health, currently does not allow me to accept payment from insurance companies; However, you can use your HSA/FSA funds to cover our services. We also do not submit service fee claims to insurance companies or use CPT codes for our procedures or medications. By signing up and requesting care as one of my patients, you acknowledge that we do not treat Medicare or Medicaid covered patients. You also acknowledge that you do not have any state-funded insurance. Individuals with state-funded insurance must seek care from their local in-person healthcare provider.

I charge a single flat fee of $99 for my services, consultation, and for a prescription. This charge is incurred every time a prescription is written.

What if insurance wont cover my medication?

While we will check your non-state funded insurance plan for medication coverage, sometimes the insurance may deny the medication. Once a determination is received by your insurance company regarding a denial of coverage for a drug, we still have a few options left. Depending on the drug I may be able to prescribe it to you at a fixed out of pocket rate and have it shipped to your doorstep!

This result varies depending on the requested medication.

Do you offer compound medication options?

Yes! When it comes to weight loss I offer compounds such as Semaglutide and Tirzepatide! These medications can be shipped to your doorstep at no extra charge, if this route is medically appropriate, complete with administration and storage instructions.
